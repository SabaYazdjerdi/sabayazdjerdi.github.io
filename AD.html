Providence exchange Gateway Station

The Providence train station is a building that has a connective association with the surrounding dense kinetic movements carried throughout; this being the movement of trains, cars, and human beings. The goal of this project was to create an architecture that would express this dense movements, with the word “density” itself being the driver of the design.

Using folding as a method for spatial generation, this architecture dynamically follows the density of the movements by the visitors through out the site, finally opening up to Providence model space in the middle. This central space conveys a very mysterious impression revealing city views and programatic interior spaces. Simultaneously, the simple language of the folded form enables a tight connection between interior and exterior.

The structural integrity of the folds become more than a method of making, it became a driver of form and concept.
