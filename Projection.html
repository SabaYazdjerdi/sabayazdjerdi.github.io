Architectural Projection

Technique of reproducing a spatial object/still life upon a plane [drawings of the objects] or a curved surface [projection of drawings back on the still life objects] or a line by projecting its points [measurement of the object].

All of these gathered data through reproduction process are stored and transformed digitally.
